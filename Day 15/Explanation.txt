Aaj maine ek simple aur clean webpage banaya hai University of Chitral ke liye, jisme maine HTML aur internal 
CSS dono use kiye. Page me ek header, navigation bar, banner image, main content cards, aur footer shamil hain.

Sabse pehle, header me university ka logo aur do links (“Email” aur “Jobs”) diye gaye hain. Header ko maine 
display: flex; justify-content: space-between; se style kiya taa keh logo left side aur links right side me 
barabar align ho jayein.

Uske baad, ek navigation bar banaya gaya hai purple background ke saath, jisme six links diye gaye hain,, 
Home, About, Admissions, Academics, Research, aur Contact. Jab mouse in links pe jata hai, to ye underline 
ho jaate hain (:hover effect).

Phir aata hai ek banner section, jisme maine admission image lagayi hai. Ye image poori width me stretch hoti 
hai (width: 100%) aur object-fit: cover; se neatly fit hoti hai.

Main content ke andar maine do rows banaye hain jisme cards hain — ek “Welcome Message” aur “VC Message”, aur 
doosri row me “Latest News” aur “Upcoming Events”. In rows ko maine Flexbox ke through design kiya 
(display: flex; flex-wrap: wrap;) taa keh ye automatically adjust ho jayein jab screen chhoti ho. 
Har card ke andar white background, light border, aur halki si shadow di gayi hai taake design soft 
aur professional lage.

Page ke end me footer diya gaya hai jisme university ka address, phone number aur kuch footer links 
(About, Contact, Privacy Policy) diye gaye hain. Footer ka background purple hai aur text white. 
Links pe hover karne se underline effect aata hai.

CSS me maine sabse upar global reset diya (margin: 0; padding: 0; box-sizing: border-box;) aur font family Arial set ki poore page ke liye
