styles sirf is component tak limited rahe, aur koi conflict na ho.

Is project ke baad mujhe samajh aaya ke React mein components aur props milkar code ko modular, clean aur reusable bana 
dete hain. Yeh concept real-world apps ke liye bohot zaroori hai, kyunki har cheez ko alag-alag component mein tod kar
